About Us

FEATURED SERVICES

MukNamz New Dawn Worldwide Inmates Initiative is a pioneering and socially conscious organization dedicated to reforming the lives of inmates, providing them with opportunities for personal growth, rehabilitation, and reintegration into society. Established in 2023, MukNamz is committed to making a positive impact on the lives of incarcerated individuals globally. Our mission is to facilitate their transformation and prepare them for a brighter, crime-free future.

MukNamz envisions a world where inmates are not merely incarcerated but given the tools, guidance, and support they need to become productive, law-abiding citizens upon release. We aim to create a global network of rehabilitated individuals who contribute positively to society.

History Timeline

  • 2001 - HR Prince Mohamed Al-Faisal launch the project to develop the Kind.
  • 2001 - Laying of foundations for the new Kind.
  • 2002- Work begins on the new development
  • 2004 - The Community Centre opens, adding over 8300m2 of usable space to the adjacent Kind.
  • 2009 - Construction begins on the Maryam Centre; a dedicated space and building for women's services.
  • 2014 - Opening of the Community Centre, making the Kind the largest Kind in the country.
